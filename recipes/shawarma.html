shawarma

This is a traditional middle eastern dish called shawarma

its mostly consisting of seasoned chicken or beef cut from a grilled skewer, peta bread and sauce

ingredients

  • seasoned chicken
  • pita bread
  • garlic sauce
  • french fries
    steps
  1. put the seasoned chicken/beef on scewer
  2. grill till well done and cut
  3. lay down peta bread, put sauce and french fries and shawarma
