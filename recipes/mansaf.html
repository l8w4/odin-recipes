mansaf

This is a very famous national dish in Jordan

this dish mainly consists of milk solids, called jameed, rice and meat

ingredients

  • meat
  • rice
  • seasonings
  • pita bread

steps

  1. boils the meat with seasonings and jameed till its almost ready
  2. cook the rice with seasonings
  3. (optional) add nuts when rice is served
  4. add a couple of spoons of the meat soup to the rice
